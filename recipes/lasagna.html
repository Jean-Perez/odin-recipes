Lasagna Recipe



Description:

The All recipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable,
so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cook time in oven: 2 hrs 30 mins
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
